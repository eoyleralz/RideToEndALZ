How It Works

1. REGISTER

Choose a ride location and commit to making a difference. We’ll be here to support you from the moment you register until you cross the finish line.

2. RAISE $ FOR RESEARCH

We’ll provide tools to make fundraising easy, and 100 percent of the dollars you raise will go toward efforts to discover methods of treatment and prevention for Alzheimer’s.

3. GO THE DISTANCE!

You are driven by determination to end this disease. Your participation helps moves us toward the first survivor of Alzheimer’s.

There are three ways you can be involved with our mission:

Rider Crew Volunteer

Show your commitment to end Alzheimer’s from your aerobars. Whether a casual cyclist or an avid enthusiast, this fully supported, one-day ride offers multiple route options. Select a distance that pushes you to perform your best, while raising funds and awareness to advance Alzheimer’s research.

Learn More

You don’t have to be on two wheels to be at the heart of the action!. From route marshalls to bike mechanics, our volunteer crew members make ride day a success.
